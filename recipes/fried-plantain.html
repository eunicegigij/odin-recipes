Fried Plantain (Dodo)

Description

Soft, sweet and juicy. This fried plantain recipe is so quick and easy to prepare plus you only need two ingredients to make it! Plantain is a simple, versatile dish that can be served as a side with lunch, dinner or as a main with beans, eggs, stew and more.

Not much needs to be said as this meal is most definitely a crowd favourite. With it being the easiest to prepare, fried plaintain is a ranked high on most Nigerians list of favourites.

ingredients

  • Ripe plantain
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. First, you will need to cut the plantain diagonally. This shape is the easiest shape to fry and is visually appealing. Other options are to cut your plantains into circles or cubes.
  2. Once your plantain is cut into pieces, heat your oil in a deep fryer or pan.
  3. Add your plantains and fry for 3-5 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  4. Once your plantain is fried, remove it from the oil and place on a paper towel so the oil is absorbed.
  5. Serve sweet fried plantain on its own or with Jollof rice, fried rice, dundun and more!
  6. Enjoy!
